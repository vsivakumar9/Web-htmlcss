Summary: Latitude vs Weather Variables

Visualizations

This is a summary of the Weather anaylsis for about 500 cities in varying latitudes covering the world. The OpenWeatherMap API was used to determine weather realated information like Maximum temperature, Humidity, cloudiness, Windspeed. After assembling the dataset pandas was used to convert to dataframes and perform needed operations. Matplotlib and Searborn was then used to plot the results of the analysis of latitude versus the other weather variables. This site provides the data and the visualizations created as part of the analysis. Observations and explanations of trends and correlations are also included.
